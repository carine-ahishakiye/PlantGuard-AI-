The Technology Behind PlantGuard

PlantGuard uses MobileNetV2 Transfer Learning fine-tuned on 54,000+ leaf images across 38 disease categories from the PlantVillage dataset.

The model reached 94% validation accuracy in just 5 training epochs, engineered to run on standard hardware without expensive GPUs. A FastAPI backend serves predictions in real time.

🔒 Privacy & Data Policy

Last updated March 2026

Your data is safe with PlantGuard

Built with privacy-first principles. Images are never stored. Passwords are encrypted. You are always in control.

📋 What data is collected?

🔐 How we protect your data
  • Images are never stored. Processed in memory and immediately discarded.
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt.
  • JWT tokens expire after 24 hours.
  • No third-party cloud services. All data stays in local SQLite.
  • No advertising. Your data is never sold.
